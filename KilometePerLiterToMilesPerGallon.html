    Kilometers Per Liter vs. Miles Per Gallon

    Formula:

    kilometers per liter = miles per gallon (US) × 0.425144

    × 0.425144 =

    kilometers per liter

    miles per gallon (US) = kilometers per liter ÷ 0.425144

    ÷ 0.425144 =

    miles per gallon (US)

    History:

    Miles Per Gallon (US)

    Miles per gallon is the distance traveled in miles consuming just one gallon of fuel.

    When evaluating fuel efficiency, the more miles per gallon, the more fuel efficient a vehicle is and the less fuel consumed. Thus, the lower the mpg rating of a vehicle the less fuel it will consume.

    Miles per gallon (US) can be abbreviated as mpg (US), and are also sometimes abbreviated as mpg. For example, 1 mile per gallon (US) can be written as 1 mpg (US) or 1 mpg.


    Kilometers Per Liter

    Kilometers per liter is the distance that can be traveled in kilometers using one liter of fuel.

    Kilometers per liter can be abbreviated as km/L, and are also sometimes abbreviated as kpl or kmpl. For example, 1 kilometer per liter can be written as 1 km/L, 1 kpl, or 1 kmpl.

    In formal expressions, the slash, or solidus (/), is used to separate units used to indicate division in an expression.
